GKM FINEST MANAGEMENT INSTITUTE

GKM Finest Management Institute (formerly known as GKM Lean Management Institute) is a place to research, develop and create Made in Vietnam Finest Management Technology. In the past 12 years, GKM Finest Management Institute has transferred technology (partly or fully) to more than 1,000 businesses/organizations to improve productivity, quality, revenue and profit, creating a foundation for development. Solid growth for businesses. Typical enterprises such as: THACO Truong Hai Group, Viettel Construction Corporation (Vietel Construction), VNPT Dong Nai, Bao Minh Confectionery Company, Legroup Company, Tan Phat Equipment Company, Julie Sandlau Company, Midway Metal Company, Manutronics Company, Thygesen Vietnam Company, MK Smart Company, etc. GKM Finest Management Institute provides solutions:

  • Consulting on business restructuring according to Made in Vietnam Finest Management Technology
  • Training Consulting Technology Finest Management Made in Vietnam (partial or full)
  • Deploying Made in Vietnam Finest Management Technology to Educational, Healthcare, Public Administration Organizations,...

GKM FINEST EDUCATION INSTITUTE

GKM Finest Education Institute (GKM Education) is a place to research, develop and create Made in Vietnam Finest Education Technology. With a team of education experts and the companionship of more than 1,000 businesses, the Finest Education Institute provides solutions:

  • Deploying manner training programs, improving overall capacity for elementary, middle, and high school students in flexible training environments at the request of parents on Made in Vietnam Finest Education Technology platform
  • Deploying vocational education training programs for college and university students in flexible training environments according to customer requirements on the Made in Vietnam Finest Education Technology platform
  • Deploy overall capacity development for teachers
  • Accompanying, long-term cooperation, restructuring Enterprises and Educational Organizations according to Made in Vietnam Finest Management Technology and the Made in Vietnam Finest Education Technology platform

GKM FINEST TECHNOLOGY INSTITUTE

GKM Finest Technology Institute (GKM Tech) is a place to research, develop and create Made in Vietnam Finest Technology. With a network of Vietnamese and international experts, Finest Technology Institute provides training, consulting, and problem solving solutions on:

  • Finest R&D Technology Made in Vietnam
  • Finest Digital Transformation Technology Made in Vietnam
  • Technology for manufacturing Vietnamese products/services
